# BnBMesh — Visual Identity for Video

## Style Prompt
Modern, futuristic, minimal. Dark canvas with subtle gradient backdrops. Cyan-to-purple gradient as the brand accent (used on text, key shapes, glows). Clean sans-serif typography. Confident, punchy motion — short transitions, no slow fades. Mesh/weave motif (woven sine waves) recurs as a visual signature.

## Colors
- `#080b13` — deep canvas (background base)
- `#0f1524` — secondary canvas (cards, inset surfaces)
- `#5cebd5` — primary accent (cyan)
- `#b88bff` — secondary accent (purple)
- `#e8ecf5` — primary text (off-white)
- `#9aa4b8` — muted text
- `#667084` — dim text / dividers
- `#f5b133` — warm accent (used sparingly for stat callouts)

## Typography
- Headlines / Wordmark: `Inter` weight 800, letter-spacing -0.02em
- Body / Captions: `Inter` weight 500-600

## Motion rules
- Transitions: 0.4-0.6s, ease `power3.out` for entries, `power3.in` for exits
- Scene changes: hard cut + brief gradient flash, NO long crossfades
- Cyan glow pulses on key elements (logos, prices)
- Mesh waves animate continuously in background of headline scenes

## What NOT to Do
- No drop shadows on text (use subtle glow instead)
- No bouncy easing (`back.out`, elastic) — feels cheap
- No more than 4 colors visible in any frame at once
- No stock vector illustrations — hand-drawn SVG only (matches the logo's signature)
- No more than 2 lines of caption text on screen at once
